About Us

Learn more about Dukaane and our mission

Our Story

Dukaane was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to empower local businesses and transform the way people shop in their communities. We noticed that while large e-commerce platforms were growing, local shop owners were struggling to compete in the digital age.

Our team of passionate developers and business experts came together to create a comprehensive ecosystem that connects local shops with customers in their area, while providing shop owners with the tools they need to thrive online.

Our Mission

To create sustainable local economies by connecting communities with their neighborhood shops through technology. We believe that strong local businesses create stronger communities.

Our Values

  • Community First: We prioritize the needs of local communities and businesses.
  • Innovation: We continuously improve our technology to better serve our users.
  • Transparency: We believe in open and honest communication with all stakeholders.
  • Empowerment: We provide tools that empower shop owners to grow their businesses.

Privacy Policy

How we protect and use your information

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect depend on how you use our apps and services.

  • Personal Information: When you create an account, we collect information like your name, email address, phone number, and location.
  • Transaction Information: We collect information about the purchases you make, products you view, and shops you interact with.
  • Device Information: We collect device-specific information such as hardware model, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including:

  • Processing your transactions and delivering products
  • Personalizing your experience and showing relevant content
  • Developing new services and features
  • Communicating with you about products, services, and promotions

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With shop owners to fulfill your orders
  • With delivery partners to deliver your products
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With your consent

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

General Questions

What is Dukaane?

Dukaane is a local shopping ecosystem that connects customers with neighborhood shops through our suite of mobile apps. We help local businesses go digital and provide convenient shopping experiences for customers.

Is Dukaane available in my city?

We're continuously expanding to new cities. Currently, we're available in major metropolitan areas across India. Check our app to see if services are available in your location.

How much does it cost to use Dukaane?

Downloading and using our apps is free for customers. Shop owners pay a small commission on successful transactions. Delivery partners earn money for each delivery they complete.

Customer Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply open the Dukaane app, browse or search for products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can choose delivery or pickup options based on what the shop offers.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and cash on delivery (where available).

What if I receive a wrong or damaged item?

If you receive an incorrect or damaged product, please contact our support team within 24 hours of delivery. We'll help resolve the issue with the shop owner.

Shop Owner Guide

Everything you need to know to get your shop online

Getting Started as a Shop Owner

Welcome to Dukaane for Shop Owners! Follow these steps to get your business online:

  1. Download the "Local Shop Manager" app from the App Store or Google Play
  2. Create your business account with your shop details
  3. Verify your business with the required documents
  4. Set up your product catalog by adding items, prices, and images
  5. Configure your delivery areas and operating hours
  6. Go live and start receiving orders!

Managing Orders

Once your shop is live, you'll start receiving orders through the app. Here's how to manage them effectively:

  • Order Notifications: You'll receive push notifications for new orders
  • Order Processing: Accept or reject orders based on your current capacity
  • Preparation Time: Set realistic preparation times for different products
  • Order Updates: Keep customers informed about their order status

Tips for Success

  • Keep your product catalog updated with accurate prices and availability
  • Use high-quality images for your products
  • Respond promptly to customer inquiries and order requests
  • Offer promotions to attract new customers
  • Ask satisfied customers for reviews to build credibility

Delivery Partner Guide

How to become a successful Dukaane delivery partner

Getting Started as a Delivery Partner

Join our network of delivery partners and start earning with flexible hours:

  1. Download the "Dukaane Delivery Partner" app
  2. Complete the registration process with your personal and vehicle details
  3. Submit the required documents for verification
  4. Complete the orientation and training process
  5. Start accepting delivery requests and earn money!

Managing Deliveries

Once you're active on the platform, follow these best practices for successful deliveries:

  • Accepting Requests: You'll receive delivery requests based on your location and availability
  • Pickup Process: Verify the order details with the shop owner before leaving
  • Delivery Process: Follow the navigation to the customer's location and complete the delivery
  • Payment Collection: Collect cash payments if applicable and confirm in the app

Maximizing Your Earnings

  • Work during peak hours (lunch and dinner times) for more delivery requests
  • Maintain a high acceptance rate to get priority for requests
  • Provide excellent service to earn tips and better ratings
  • Keep your vehicle in good condition to avoid downtime
  • Familiarize yourself with your delivery area to optimize routes
