Our Story

Dukaane was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to empower local businesses and make shopping more convenient for customers. We noticed that while large e-commerce platforms were growing, local kirana stores were struggling to compete in the digital age.

Our team of passionate entrepreneurs, technologists, and business experts came together to create a platform that would help local shops go online while providing customers with the convenience of doorstep delivery.

Our Mission

To create sustainable local economies by connecting communities with their neighborhood shops through technology. We believe that strong local businesses create stronger communities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Dukaane

What is Dukaane?

Dukaane is a hyperlocal online grocery delivery platform that connects customers with local kirana stores, vegetable vendors, and fruit shops. We provide fast same-day delivery of fresh groceries and daily essentials.

How fast is delivery?

We offer same-day delivery, with delivery times ranging from 30 minutes to 2 hours depending on your location and store availability. You can choose your preferred delivery slot during checkout.

Is cash on delivery available?

Yes, we offer cash on delivery as well as online payment options including UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and digital wallets. You can choose your preferred payment method during checkout.

How do I become a delivery partner?

Download the Dukaane Delivery Partner app from our website, complete the registration process with required documents, and you can start accepting delivery requests. Flexible hours and good earnings.

How can my shop join Dukaane?

Download the Dukaane Shop Manager app, register your store with basic details and documents, set up your product catalog, and you can start receiving orders. There's no registration fee for shop owners.

What if I receive damaged or wrong items?

If you receive damaged or incorrect items, please contact our support team within 24 hours of delivery. We'll help resolve the issue with the store owner and ensure you get the correct items or a refund.

Is Dukaane available in my city?

We're currently available in major cities across India including Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, and Kolkata. We're expanding rapidly to cover more cities. Check our Cities page for updates.

How much does it cost to use Dukaane?

Downloading and using our apps is completely free for customers. Shop owners pay a small commission on successful transactions. Delivery partners earn money for each delivery they complete.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect depend on how you use our apps and services.

  • Personal Information: When you create an account, we collect information like your name, email address, phone number, and location.
  • Transaction Information: We collect information about the purchases you make, products you view, and shops you interact with.
  • Device Information: We collect device-specific information such as hardware model, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Location Information: With your permission, we collect precise location data to show you nearby stores and enable delivery services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including:

  • Processing your transactions and delivering products
  • Personalizing your experience and showing relevant content
  • Developing new services and features
  • Communicating with you about products, services, and promotions
  • Ensuring the security and integrity of our platform
  • Complying with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Shop Owners: To fulfill your orders, we share necessary information like delivery address and contact details with the shop owner.
  • With Delivery Partners: To deliver your products, we share delivery information with our delivery partners.
  • Service Providers: We work with third-party service providers who help us operate our platform (payment processors, hosting services, etc.).
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights, we may disclose information to law enforcement or other authorities.
  • With Your Consent: We share information when you give us explicit consent to do so.
